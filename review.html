Frequently Asked Questions

Is Otto actually a pet insurance company?

No, Otto Insurance is not a pet insurance company. It's a lead generation service that connects you with actual pet insurance providers like Progressive, State Farm, GEICO, and others. This is a crucial distinction that's often misunderstood in marketing materials.

How much does Otto pet insurance cost?

Otto doesn't set prices since they're not an insurance company. The actual costs depend on which insurer you're connected with through their platform. Based on their partner network, expect monthly premiums ranging from $30-80+ for dogs, depending on age, breed, location, and coverage level.

Does Otto cover pre-existing conditions?

Otto doesn't provide coverage themselves, so pre-existing condition coverage depends on the actual insurance provider you're matched with. Most traditional pet insurers that Otto works with exclude pre-existing conditions, following industry standards.

What's the Otto insurance claims process like?

Since Otto is a referral service, you'd file claims directly with the insurance company they connected you with. Each provider has their own claims process and turnaround times. Otto provides no claims support since they don't underwrite policies.

Should I use Otto to find pet insurance?
